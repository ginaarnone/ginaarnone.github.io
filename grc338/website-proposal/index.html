drip cafe

drip cafe is a local coffee shop serving premium-quality coffee and freshly baked donuts. It's a place where workers can re-energize, where creatives can design and collaborate, and where strangers can become new friends. As the only solely organically-sourced coffee shop in the area, it attracts people from all over the community as well as visiting tourists.

The shop's target audience is primarily students, creatives, and local workers.

The site and shop will likely be primarily frequented by students, workers, creatives, and visitors in the area looking for quality/organic coffee. Needs to be mobile-friendly as younger people frequently look up menus/businesses on their phones when on the go.

drip cafe's goals are to provide a high-quality and sustainable coffee alternative as well as to operate as a place for the community to rest, energize, work, collaborate, and meet new people.
