ARTECHOUSE NYC Takes Us on a "Machine Hallucination"

At the ARTECHOUSE in New York, Turkish artist Rafik Anadol is showing “Machine Hallucination” — a synthetic reality exhibition that utilizes an array of high-tech advancements to display 100 million architectural images of New York City. These images reveal the hidden connections between the iconic skyline, while offering a hallucinatory look into the Artificial Intelligence (AI) machine that processes these images
